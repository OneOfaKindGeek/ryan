My Navy Life

Kind of a biography
by Ryan Larson

Why I joined

I joined becasue leaving high school I had no idea what I wanted to do for a career and student loans terrfied me. So joining the navy seemed like solid choice to earn some life experience and decide on what I really wanted to do or stay in my entire life.

Why I left

I left because it honestly was not something I wanted to do for the rest of my life and nearing the end of my career I felt drained, so I left to go to college and find a new career.
